What is the COVID-19 pandemic?


This is a pandemic caused by coronavirus where the symptoms of this virus include loss of taste and smell along with a fever and many other symptoms.The first symptoms are observed within 14 days of getting exposed to the virus and this could lead to more severe symptoms such as hypoxia or even respiratory failure. First case of this virus was made known in Wuhan, China in late 2019 and it had since spread to many parts of the world.
