Changes To Academic Experiences

We decided to conduct a study to discover what changes to the academic experience students have noticed.

5 Questions were asked:

  1. What year of academic study are you in?
  2. Is online learning more or less engaging?
  3. Do they prefer online or in person lectures?
  4. Do they prefer online or in person assessments?
  5. What have been the changes to their learning experience due to online classes?

Results


We had 16 anonymous responses with each participant answering all questions

88% were in their first year of study, 6%(1person) were undergraduates but not in first year, 6% were postgraduate

94% said online learning is less enaging with only 1 person saying its more engaging

88% said they prefer in person to online classes, and 2 people saying the prefer online classes

81% said they prefer online assessments, with 3 people saying the prefer in person assessments

The last question let people type their answer in the box
