Timeline of covid-19 pandemic

  • 31st December 2019

    A series of cases of pneumonia had been reported in Wuhan,China.This later turned out to be a new virus identified as a coronavirus where there was no evidence of human transmission

  • 11th January 2020

    First death due to the virus of a 61 year old man who frequented Huanan seafood market was confirmed in Wuhan.The virus also spread to other countries such as Thailand and Japan.

  • 30th January 2020

    The virus is declared a global health emergency by the world health organisation.

  • February 23rd 2020

    Italy reaches around 150 cases as there had been a rise in cases.This made it the first place in Europe where a major outbreak occured.This lead to many sport events and schools to be closed as 10 towns entered lockdown.

  • March 17th 2020

    The European Union had placed a ban on most visitors travelling outside the bloc from 26 countries as a response to the pandemic.

  • April 2nd 2020

    The global death toll due to the pandemic reaches 51,000 and the number of infections surpass 1 million

  • September 28th 2020

    The global death toll due to the pandemic surpasses 1 million and the number of infections surpass 30 million.

  • December 2nd 2020

    The UK approves the pfizner vaccine which was developed by an American pharmaceutical company BioNTech.
